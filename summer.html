Prague, Czech Republic

please tag along

Prague is a city that captivates with its rich history and stunning architecture. From Gothic to Baroque, the buildings showcase a blend of styles that tell the story of the city. We loved wandering the narrow cobblestone streets, enjoying classical concerts, and immersing ourselves in the vibrant nightlife. The squares and cafes are alive with activity, making Prague a must-visit for a culturally rich summer experience. Historical landmarks abound, highlighting the city’s fascinating past. Prague Castle, one of the largest ancient castles in the world, offers breathtaking views and a deep dive into the history of the Czech Republic. The Charles Bridge, adorned with Baroque statues, is especially enchanting at sunrise or sunset, providing magical views. Don't miss the Astronomical Clock in Old Town Square, a medieval marvel that puts on a captivating show every hour. For art enthusiasts, the city boasts an array of museums and galleries, including the National Gallery and the Museum of Decorative Arts, showcasing a diverse range of art from various periods.

Spain, Barcelona

please tag along

Spain, situated in southwestern Europe, is celebrated for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes. The capital, Madrid, acts as a vibrant cultural hub, while Barcelona captivates visitors with its unique architecture and dynamic arts scene. The country’s natural beauty is evident in its Mediterranean beaches, the rugged Pyrenees and Sierra Nevada mountains, and the vast plains of La Mancha. Spain's history is a tapestry woven from Roman and Moorish influences, along with a golden age of exploration. Iconic landmarks such as the Alhambra and the Sagrada Família, alongside the passionate art of flamenco music and dance, further enhance Spain's charm.
