Cairns, Australia

please tag along

Cairns, the gateway to Queensland's tropical north, is a relaxed city best experienced outdoors. This region is renowned for the iconic Great Barrier Reef and the Wet Tropics World Heritage Rain forest, but the city itself is also worth a visit. You'll discover vibrant cafés, lively markets, and beautiful nearby beaches. Whether you choose to unwind by a resort pool or spend your days exploring this tropical paradise, Cairns has something for everyone.

Queenstown, New Zealand

please tag along

Queenstown is one of New Zealand's top destinations for thrilling, adrenaline-pumping adventures. Activities like paragliding, bungy jumping, jet boating, skydiving, hiking, skiing, and mountain biking are among the highlights of what this vibrant town has to offer. If you prefer to stay on solid ground, there are plenty of options as well. You can hike scenic trails, enjoy shopping, take a picturesque drive along Lake Wakatipu, unwind in a hot pool, or savor the delights of Queenstown's excellent restaurants and bars. Additionally, Queenstown serves as a fantastic base for exploring the surrounding region, with day trips available to Milford Sound, Arrowtown, and nearby wine regions.
